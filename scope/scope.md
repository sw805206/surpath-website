# scope.md

## What is Surpath
Surpath (SP) is a 3PL logistics company specializing in e-Commerce fulfillment. SP moves, stores, and ships products for online sellers.

## Core Services
- Receive and putaway products at warehouse
- Order management via API integration or manual entry
- Pick, pack, label, and ship orders
- Last mile delivery via FedEx, UPS, USPS, and other carriers
- Transportation: ocean, air, trucking, courier

## Specializations
- Standard products: 5–25 lbs
- Heavy/bulky products: 25–100 lbs
- Licensed to store and ship DG (Dangerous Goods) / lithium battery products

## Network
- HQ: Brea, CA (US) and Shanghai (China)
- 30+ warehouses at major US gateways
- Geographic coverage: US, Canada | China (Pearl River Delta, Yangtze Delta) | Southeast Asia

## Value-Added Services (VAS)
Surpath operates a network of trusted partners offering VAS to e-Commerce sellers. VAS details to be added when the VAS page is built.

## Target Customers
- Amazon sellers
- Multi-channel sellers
- Brand owners
Primary audience: US-based sellers who source products from China.

## Differentiators (in priority order)
1. Last mile cost savings — 75–80% below carriers' published rates
2. 30+ warehouse network at all major US gateways
3. Free supply chain design consultation (included with services)
4. Wide network of trusted VAS partners
5. Licensed DG / lithium battery capability

## Unique Positioning
Free supply chain consulting and a curated VAS partner network are not typical 3PL offerings. These are the primary differentiators beyond cost and network scale.
